Involvement

Cal Club Golf is a competitive team that represents UC Berkeley in the National Collegiate Club Golf Association. As Founder and President, I established the club in Fall 2013 through a series of proposals to the Sports Club Department. During practices every week and other pastimes, members of the team have a great time following through with their passion for golf and hanging out with fellow golfers.

The Regents' and Chancellor's Scholar Association is a group of Cal students who were accepted to UC Berkely and given the Regents' and Chancellor's Scholarship. As a part of the association, it is great meeting people of diverse backgrounds and of different passions. Interacting with graduating seniors, faculty mentors, and others through the organization has been extremely eye-opening and rewarding.

The Delta Chi Fraternity is a social fraternity registered with UC Berkeley. Brothers of the fraternity get along great with each other and become very close friends, whether you live in the Delta Chi house or not. Through this group, I have found both a sensible and smart group of friends who know how to have an awesome time.

Home of Hope Youth is a charity that supports educational efforts for destitute children in India. As Co-founder, I helped incentivize high schoolers to become project leaders. Among the variety of programs, there is the Crafts for Hope inititative and the Pen Pal correspondence. All the members really enjoy taking an active role in changing the lives of children across the globe.
